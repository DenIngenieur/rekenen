🧮 Math Exercises – plus, minus, times, divide

What is this?

An online math game to practice mental arithmetic. Choose an operation and solve a series of sums. After each answer, you will see facts that show how the sum relates to other operations (for example, inverse operations).

What can you do with it?

  • Choose an operation on the start screen:
    + - × ÷ or combinations like + / –, × / ÷ or mix (everything mixed up).
  • Set your own limits for how high the numbers can go (max) and how many questions you want.
  • Audio feedback (toggle on/off with the speaker icon).
  • After every answer you see:
    • whether it was correct or incorrect (green or red background)
    • three "facts" that rewrite the same numbers differently (e.g., 4 + 3 = 7 → 7 – 3 = 4)
  • Keyboard navigation: press the right arrow key → for the next sum.

How do you use it?

  1. Go to the homepage.
  2. Click on a tile with the operation you want to practice.
  3. Choose the maximum number (e.g., 20) and the number of questions (e.g., 5).
  4. Click the green start button (🚀 ▶).
  5. Type your answer in the box and click OK (or press Enter).
  6. Look at the explanation (facts) and then click ▶ (or right arrow) for the next sum.
  7. After the last sum, you will see your score: correct / total.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes, the page adapts to any screen (mobile, tablet, laptop).

Troubleshooting?

  • No sound? Turn on the sound using the speaker icon and check if your browser allows audio for this site.
  • Images not loading? Refresh the page – all images are included.

Have fun practicing! 🎉
